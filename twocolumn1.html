Naruto

Twelve years ago the nine tailed fox attacked the Hidden Leaf village. In order to stop the fox, the fourth hokage sacrificed his life to seal the fox's spirit inside Naruto Uzumaki. For that reason Naruto is shunned by the villagers and he searches for the purpose of his life. Wanting to become acknowledged, Naruto vows to become Hokage someday so that everyone will respect him. Along the way he finds new friends and becomes the village's number one most knuckle-headed ninja.

DragonBall Z

Dragonball Z is an sequel to the anime: Dragonball. Dragonball Z is a story about an adult named Goku who goes through many different adventures and saving the Earth countless times from powerful opponents like intergalactic space fighters, androids, or magical creatures. Dragon Ball Z shows the growth of Goku, his son, and many other characters from Dragonball or Dragbonall Z.

One Piece(Ongoing serie)

The story begins with the King of the Pirates(Gol D.Roger) being killed, just before his death he announces that he treasure the One Piece is free to anyone who can find it. 22 years later a young man named Luffy who's body is made of rubber, is inspired by his childhood idol: the pirate Red Hair Shanks to find the One Piece and become the King of the Pirates. Luffy sets off with his diverse crew and finds himself in many adventures along his way to becoming the King of the Pirates.

Sword Art Online

Sword Art Online is a virtual reality game that was released in 2022 along with the Nerve Gear. The Nerve Gear is a helmet that stimulates the brain in you 5 senses. When players log into SAO for the first time, they realize they are unable to log out. Akihiko Kayaba(The creator of SAO) tells them that if they wish to log out, they need to beat the game. This anime follows Kirito's efforts(1 of the beta testers) and many other players in order to beat the game.
